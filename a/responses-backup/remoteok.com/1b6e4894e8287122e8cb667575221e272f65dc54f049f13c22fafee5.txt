You will represent Carta as a valuable contributor to the growing venture ecosystem across Carta’s network.&nbsp;\n\nAbout You\n\nYou enjoy building lasting, productive relationships. You are curious, and you’re driven to key in on relationships and strategies that benefit all sides. You dig in to find ways to work with others. You are data-informed.
